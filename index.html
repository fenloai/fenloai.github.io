When AI Actually Makes Sense

AI is worth exploring if you:

  • Answer the same questions over and over from customers or staff
  • Spend hours searching through documents for answers
  • Have staff doing repetitive data entry or copy-paste work
  • Can't get insights from your own spreadsheets and data
  • Want to scale without hiring proportionally
