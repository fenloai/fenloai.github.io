Common Questions

Do you work with my industry?

We work with any SMB that uses software. If your tools have APIs, we can connect them. Some industries we know well: professional services, e-commerce, healthcare admin, and real estate.

What if I don't know which tier I need?

Most businesses think they need an AI agent when they actually need automation first. Book a free diagnostic — we'll tell you honestly where to start.

Do you maintain what you build?

Yes. Monthly maintenance is 10% of the build cost. Includes monitoring, bug fixes, and minor tweaks. You're free to take it in-house anytime.

Who owns the code?

You do. Always. We hand over all source code, credentials, and documentation. No vendor lock-in. No proprietary black boxes.

What if we're not happy with the result?

We do weekly check-ins during the build. If we're off track, we fix it then. We're a small team — we can't afford unhappy clients.

Can you work with our existing developers?

Absolutely. We can build standalone systems or integrate with your existing stack. We'll hand off clean, documented code your team can take over.
