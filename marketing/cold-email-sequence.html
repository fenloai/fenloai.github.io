Rules Before You Send

  • Personalize every email. Mention something specific about their business (website, recent post, job listing).
  • Use your real name and email. No "noreply."
  • If they reply "not interested," reply once with "Fair enough — mind if I check back in 6 months?" Then stop.
  • Track in a spreadsheet. Don't blast. Max 20–50/week.
  • Send Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM in their timezone.
  • Click any email to expand/collapse. Use the Copy button to grab the full text.
