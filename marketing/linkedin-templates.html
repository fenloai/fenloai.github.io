LinkedIn Outreach Templates — Connection Requests, DMs, Comments

Rules Before You Connect

  • Send 10–15 connection requests per week max.
  • Never pitch in the connection request.
  • Engage with their content for 1–2 weeks before any ask.
  • If they don't accept within 7 days, move on.
  • Track in a spreadsheet.
  • Click any template to expand/collapse. Use the Copy button to grab the full text.

Connection Request Templates

5 variants — never pitch in the request

The Industry Peer
Generic ▼
Hi [First Name], I work with [industry] businesses on operational automation. Came across your profile — would be great to connect and learn what you're building at [Company Name].
The Content Engager
Engagement ▼
Hi [First Name], enjoyed your recent post on [topic]. Working on similar challenges in [industry]. Would love to connect.
The Mutual Interest
Generic ▼

Local ▼

Direct Message Templates

4 stages: Value Share → Soft Ask → Direct Ask → Breakup

DM 1: The Value Share
Day 3 ▼
Hey [First Name], thanks for connecting. No pitch — wrote something that might be relevant to your work at [Company Name]: [link to blog post] Thought you'd find it useful. [Your Name]
DM 2: The Soft Ask
Day 7 ▼
Hey [First Name], Quick question: what's the most repetitive task your team deals with right now? Not fishing for a sales call. Genuinely curious what [industry] operators are dealing with. [Your Name]
DM 3: The Direct Ask
Day 10+ ▼
Hey [First Name], That [specific task they mentioned] is exactly what we build automations for. Don't know if it makes sense for [Company Name] — depends on volume and tools. Happy to do a free 10-minute audit and tell you honestly either way. Worth a quick call? https://fenloai.com/pages/book-discovery.html [Your Name]
DM 3b: The Breakup
No reply ▼
Hey [First Name], I won't keep following up. If [Company Name] ever needs to audit a repetitive workflow, I'm here. 10 minutes. I'll tell you honestly if automation makes sense or if you should wait. https://fenloai.com/pages/book-discovery.html [Your Name]

Comment Templates

Use these to engage on prospects' posts before sending a connection request

On a growth/scale post
Growth ▼
"This resonates. We're seeing the same thing with [industry] clients — processes that worked at 10 people break at 30. What's been the hardest operational shift for your team?"
On a hiring post
Hiring ▼
"Congrats on the growth. Are you hiring for [role] because the current process is breaking, or because you're expanding into new areas? We see both, and the fix is usually different."
On a customer service post
Operations ▼
"This is exactly why we build automations for [industry] businesses. The 5-minute response rule is great in theory, but impossible manually at scale. What tools are you using for [specific process]?"
On a general business post
General ▼
"Solid perspective. We're working with similar challenges — specifically around [topic]. Would love to hear what's working for your team."

Profile Optimization Checklist

Update your LinkedIn profile before starting outreach:

  • Headline: "Helping [industry] businesses automate repetitive work | Fenlo AI"
  • About section: 2-3 paragraphs. Mention honest approach, fixed pricing, team size. Link to services page and blog.
  • Featured section: Pin 2-3 blog posts or the Services page
  • Banner image: Simple text — "Automation for [industry] | Fixed pricing | fenloai.com"
  • Activity: Turn ON "Share profile updates with your network"
  • Creator mode: Enable (shows "Follow" instead of "Connect" for non-1st degree)
