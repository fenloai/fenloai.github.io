# Fenlo AI - Company Profile & Marketing Foundation

**Fenlo AI** is a boutique AI consulting and implementation firm focused exclusively on small businesses. We help businesses leverage artificial intelligence to solve real operational problems - but only when it makes sense.

## Our Core Philosophy

We believe in **honest assessments over aggressive sales**. We will tell you "no" if AI isn't the right solution for your business. This approach builds trust and ensures every client we work with sees genuine results.

| Differentiator | What It Means |

| **Honest Assessments** | We decline projects when AI isn't the right fit. We'd rather lose a sale than waste your money. |
| **Fixed Pricing** | No hourly billing surprises. You know the cost upfront before we start. |
| **Rapid Implementation** | Simple solutions in 1-2 weeks, complex solutions in 2-3 weeks. No dragging projects out. |
