# Fenlo AI - Marketing Execution Plan
## Cold Outreach + Company LinkedIn Strategy (Zero Budget)

**Constraint:** 3 co-founders with other jobs - cannot use personal LinkedIn profiles for promotion.

---

## Revised Strategy Overview

Since personal LinkedIn promotion isn't an option, we'll shift to:

1. **Cold Email Outreach (Primary)** - 70% of effort
2. **LinkedIn Company Page** - 20% of effort
3. **Referral Partnerships** - 10% of effort

This approach doesn't require personal branding or daily social media presence.

### Company Page Optimization

**Limitations to Accept:**
- Company pages get 10-20x less reach than personal profiles
- Can't send connection requests from company pages
- Harder to build followers organically

**How to Work Around This:**
- Focus on credibility, not reach (prospects will check your page after cold outreach)
- Post 2-3x per week (not daily - saves time)
- Content serves as "trust verification" when prospects research you

## Part 1: Cold Email Outreach (Primary Channel)

Cold email is your main lead generation engine. It doesn't require personal branding, works while you sleep, and can be done in batches on weekends.

### Target Niches (Start with 3)

| Niche | Pain Point | Decision Maker |
|-------|------------|----------------|
| Law Firms (5-30 people) | Repetitive client questions, document search | Managing Partner, Office Manager |
| Medical Practices | Patient inquiries, appointment FAQs | Practice Manager, Owner |
| Real Estate Agencies | Property questions, lead qualification | Broker/Owner, Operations Manager |

### Finding Prospects (Free Methods)

1. **LinkedIn Search:** "[Title]" + "[Industry]" + "[Location]"
2. **Google:** "[Industry] [City]" → Visit websites → Find contact info
3. **Hunter.io:** 25 free email lookups/month
4. **Apollo.io:** 50 free credits/month
5. **Company websites:** Check About/Team/Contact pages

### Daily Outreach Targets
- **Cold emails:** 20-30 per day
- **LinkedIn connections:** 20-30 per day
- **Follow-ups:** As scheduled

---

### Cold Email Templates

#### Email 1: The Pattern Interrupt (Day 1)
```
Subject: Quick question about [Company Name]

Hi [First Name],

How many times a day does someone at [Company Name] answer the same client question?

We help [industry] businesses automate repetitive inquiries with AI - but honestly, it's not right for everyone.

Would a 15-minute call to see if it makes sense for you be worth it? No pitch, just an honest assessment.

[Your Name]
Fenlo AI
fenloai.com
```

#### Email 2: Value Add (Day 4)
```
Subject: Re: Quick question about [Company Name]

Hi [First Name],

Quick follow-up.

I put together a simple checklist we use to determine if AI actually makes sense for a small business.

Want me to send it over? Takes 2 minutes to review.

[Your Name]
```

#### Email 3: Breakup (Day 8)
```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi [First Name],

I've reached out a couple times about AI automation for [Company Name].

If it's not relevant right now, no problem - I'll stop reaching out.

But if you're curious whether AI could save your team time, I'm happy to do a free 15-min assessment. No strings.

Either way, wishing you well.

[Your Name]
```

## Part 2: Referral Partnership Strategy

Since you can't do personal outreach, referrals from complementary service providers are valuable.

### Target Partners (Don't Compete, Do Complement)
- Web developers/designers
- Digital marketing agencies
- Business coaches/consultants
- Accountants/bookkeepers serving small businesses
- IT service providers

### Partner Outreach Email
```
Subject: Potential referral partnership?

Hi [Name],

I run Fenlo AI - we build AI automation for small businesses (chatbots, document Q&A, workflow automation).

I noticed you work with [industry] clients. Our services don't overlap, and I thought there might be opportunities to refer clients to each other.

If your clients ever mention struggling with:
- Repetitive customer questions
- Searching through documents
- Manual data entry

...that's exactly what we solve.

Worth a quick 15-min chat to explore?

[Your Name]
Fenlo AI
```

### How to Find Partners
- Google: "[service] agency [your city]"
- LinkedIn: Search for agency owners in your area
- Local business networking groups (virtual or in-person)

## Key Reminders

1. **Consistency > Intensity** - Small effort every week beats big bursts
2. **Cold email is your engine** - This is where leads come from without personal branding
3. **Company LinkedIn = credibility** - Prospects will check it after receiving your email
4. **Personalize the first line** - Generic messages get ignored
5. **Lead with value** - Your free Executive Briefing is a competitive advantage
6. **Stay honest** - Your differentiation is trustworthiness. Never compromise it.
7. **Track everything** - Measure what works, drop what doesn't
8. **Split the work** - 3 founders = 1.5 hrs/week each
