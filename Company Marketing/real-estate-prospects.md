## Cold Email Template for Real Estate

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company Name]

Hi [First Name],

How many times a day does someone on your team answer the same property question?

"Is this home still available?"
"What are the HOA fees?"
"Can I schedule a showing?"

We help real estate agencies automate these repetitive inquiries with AI chatbots - but honestly, it's not right for everyone.

Would a 15-minute call to see if it makes sense for [Company Name] be worth it? No pitch, just an honest assessment.

[Your Name]
Fenlo AI
fenloai.com
```
