# DESIGN NOTES

## Visual Style Guidelines

**Do:**
- One idea per slide
- Large text, minimal words
- High-quality images/icons
- Consistent color scheme
- White space is good

**Don't:**
- Walls of text
- Clip art
- Busy backgrounds
- More than 6 bullet points
- Read slides verbatim

## Suggested Color Palette

- **Primary:** Dark navy or charcoal (#1a1a2e or #2d3436)
- **Accent:** Teal or blue (#00b894 or #0984e3)
- **Text:** White on dark, dark gray on light
- **Highlights:** Warm accent for emphasis (#fdcb6e)

- Flaticon
