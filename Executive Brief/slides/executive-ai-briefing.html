Honest Truths

What AI Cannot Do

AI Type Key Limitation
Predictive Only as good as historical data
Perception Struggles with edge cases
Generative Hallucinations — confidently wrong
Physical Expensive, inflexible

Universal Truths

  • Garbage in = garbage out — Data quality determines AI quality
  • AI has no judgment — Can't weigh ethical tradeoffs
  • AI doesn't understand — Pattern matching, not comprehension
  • Security matters — AI needs governance and oversight
"Knowing limitations makes you a smarter buyer and user."

Key Takeaways

If You Remember Nothing Else

1
AI is a family, not a single tool
Predictive, Perception, Generative, Physical — each solves different problems.
2
Most valuable AI is boring and proven
The hype is new; the value is established. Start with proven AI for quick wins.
3
Start with the problem, not the technology
"What hurts?" before "What's cool?" Match the AI type to your challenge.
"What would your team do with 10 extra hours per week?"
