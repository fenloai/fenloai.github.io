---------------------------------------
Generate a reference image of your protagonist (business owner) to maintain

clean modern office background slightly blurred, photorealistic, warm tones,
professional headshot style"

STEP 2: CREATE STYLE REFERENCE IMAGES
-------------------------------------
Generate 2-3 images to establish the visual tone for all scenes.

Prompt A - Office Environment:
"Modern minimalist office interior, natural daylight streaming through
large windows, clean white desk with laptop and coffee cup, green plant
accent, warm and inviting atmosphere, soft shadows, photorealistic,
architectural photography style"

Prompt B - Consultation Scene:
"Two professionals having a friendly conversation at a small round table,
coffee shop or modern office lounge, natural candid body language, warm
lighting, clean background, photorealistic, documentary style"

Prompt C - Success/Relief:
"Business professional looking relaxed and satisfied while viewing laptop
screen, genuine smile, leaning back in modern office chair, natural daylight,
clean workspace, warm color tones, candid photography style"

STYLE KEYWORDS TO USE CONSISTENTLY:
- Natural lighting, soft shadows
- Modern minimalist
- Warm tones, clean aesthetic
- Photorealistic
- Candid, not staged
- Light and airy
- Olive green accents (#6b7c3f) where appropriate

STYLE KEYWORDS TO AVOID:
- Dark, moody, dramatic lighting
- Neon colors, cyan, purple
- Futuristic, sci-fi aesthetic
- Corporate stock photo style
- Overly polished/artificial
- Heavy shadows or contrast

"You've heard the AI hype. Everyone says you need it.

But here's what most AI companies won't tell you...

Not every business needs AI. At least, not yet.

- Avoid: Sales-y tone, over-enthusiasm, dramatic pauses
