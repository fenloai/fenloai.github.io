## Case Studies: Large Enterprise

### Klarna AI Assistant (2024)

**Results in First Month:**
- Handled **2.3 million** customer service conversations
- Represented **two-thirds** of all customer service chats
- Equivalent work of **700 full-time agents**
- Customer interactions completed in **2 minutes** (down from 11 minutes)
- Available in **23 markets**, communicates in **35+ languages**
- **25%** decrease in repeat inquiries
- Estimated **$40 million USD** profit improvement for 2024
- Deployment cost: **$2-3 million**

**2025 Update:**
- AI still handles two-thirds of customer inquiries
- Response times improved by **82%**
- **25%** drop in repeat issues
- Now supports **800 FTE-equivalent** of work

**Source:** Klarna International Press Release, OpenAI Case Study
https://www.klarna.com/international/press/klarna-ai-assistant-handles-two-thirds-of-customer-service-chats-in-its-first-month/
https://openai.com/index/klarna/

### JPMorgan Chase COIN System

- Reviews **12,000** commercial credit agreements annually in seconds
- Previously consumed **360,000 hours** of lawyer and loan officer time per year
- Extracts **150 different attributes** from each contract
- Near-zero error rate, accuracy exceeding human review
- Compliance-related errors reduced by ~**80%**
- By 2017, handling **1.7 million** access requests in IT (equivalent to 140 people)

**Source:** Harvard Business School Digital Initiative
https://d3.harvard.edu/platform-rctom/submission/jp-morgan-coin-a-banks-side-project-spells-disruption-for-the-legal-industry/

### Microsoft Internal Search

- Over **6,000 hours** saved per month across 1.1 million internal searches
- **20:1 return ratio** (8,000 hours benefit vs. 300 hours invested)
- Nearly **half of all searches** utilize search administration tools
- Employees experience **1-minute faster** search completion times

**Source:** Microsoft InsideTrack
https://www.microsoft.com/insidetrack/blog/generating-great-results-administering-search-at-microsoft/
